About Me

I’m a second-year graduate student in Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology, Univerity of Chinese Academy of Science, advised by Prof. Lujia Wang.

Before starting my M.S., I received my B.S. in Network Engineering from Hainan University in 2017.

My research interest include Cloud Robotics, Agricultural Robotics and Machine Learning. I am currently researching cloud robotics at cloud computing center at SIAT. I also serve as honorary president of Hainan University Association for Robotics and Artificial Intelligence (A-RAI).
